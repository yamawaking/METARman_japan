# METARman_japan
## Overview
  **METARman_japan** shows you METAR from almost all of Japanese airport. Their locations are visualized on the map, and you can read their names and their current METAR if you click (on your PC) or tap (on your smartphone) the pin on their locations. His design, structure, and function are very simple that there is no need to worry about struggling or having trouble figuring out how to use him. You do not have to install anything but only visiting the url is enough. url: <https://yamawaking.github.io/METARman/>
